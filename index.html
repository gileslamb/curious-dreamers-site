The studio

Curious Dreamers

A Glasgow-based music and screen studio. We make animated series, immersive films, sound installations and creative education projects. Music comes first in everything we do.

Track record

Award-winning from the start

Hushabye Lullaby won double RTS awards for CBeebies and was voted one of the top 50 children's shows ever made by Guardian readers. Distance to the Moon picked up awards on the festival circuit. We set the bar high and we keep it there.

How we work

Music first. Every time.

Sound and music aren't added at the end of our process. They start it. We work across animation, live action, stop motion, and immersive formats, and we bring a European artistic sensibility to everything we touch.

Who we work with

The right people know us

BAFTA, RTS, WFTV, Screen Scotland, BFI, CBeebies, Channel 4. Broadcasters, funders, and festival programmers come to us when they want something that doesn't look like everything else.

Curious Minds

Sharing what we know

Between us we've spent decades making things, teaching people to make things, and figuring out what actually works. Curious Minds is how we share that.

We run masterclasses in soundtrack composition, screen skills, creative storytelling, and play-based practice. We work with industry professionals, educators, students, and young people.

Championing women in film and television runs through everything we do. We support female talent across directing, writing, design, and production at every stage of their career.

Play and creativity

Play is serious work. We run sessions for children, young people, and the adults who work with them, putting creative play at the centre of how we learn and make.

Screen skills and storytelling

Practical masterclasses in animation, live action, sound design, and how to tell a story through image and sound. From students to seasoned professionals.

Women in film and TV

Mentoring, advocating, and opening doors for women working across directing, writing, design, and production. This matters to us and we put real time into it.

Immersive and new forms

Galleries, theatres, online, outdoors. We're genuinely curious about what happens when you take the work off the screen and put it somewhere unexpected.
