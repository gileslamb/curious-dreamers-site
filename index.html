The studio

Curious Dreamers

A Glasgow-based music and screen studio making bold, original work — animated series, immersive film, sound installations, and creative education. Always with music and sound at its heart.

Track record

Award-winning from day one

Hushabye Lullaby won double RTS awards for CBeebies and was voted one of the top 50 children's shows ever made by Guardian readers. Distance to the Moon won multiple awards on the festival circuit. Everything we make, we make to last.

Creative approach

Music first. Always

Sound and music aren't added at the end — they shape every project from the first idea. We work across animation, live action, stop motion, and immersive formats, with a European artistic sensibility and a deep respect for the intelligence of our audiences.

Industry standing

Trusted across the sector

BAFTA, RTS, WFTV, Screen Scotland, BFI, CBeebies, Channel 4. We work with broadcasters, funders, and festival programmers who want something genuinely different — and know we'll deliver it.

Curious Minds

Sharing what we know

Our work spans animation, music, TV, film, and immersive experience — and we believe all of that knowledge belongs in the room, not just on the screen.

Curious Minds is our training and mentorship strand: masterclasses in soundtrack composition, screen skills, creative storytelling, and the art of play — for professionals, educators, students, and young people.

Central to everything we do is championing women in film and television — supporting female talent across directing, writing, design, and production at every level.
