Curious Minds

Sharing what we know

Our work spans animation, music, TV, film, and immersive experience — and we believe all of that knowledge belongs in the room, not just on the screen.

Curious Minds is our training and mentorship strand: masterclasses in soundtrack composition, screen skills, creative storytelling, and the art of play — for professionals, educators, students, and young people.

Central to everything we do is championing women in film and television — supporting female talent across directing, writing, design, and production at every level.
