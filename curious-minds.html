Curious Dreamers→Curious Minds

Between us we have decades of experience making creative work, and Curious Minds is where we share all of that, through workshops, masterclasses, and hands-on collaboration with industry, education, and young people.

We run masterclasses in soundtrack composition, screen skills, creative storytelling, and play-based practice. We work with industry professionals, educators, students, and young people, in studios, schools, festivals, and online.

Championing women in film and television runs through everything we do. We support female talent across directing, writing, design, and production at every stage of their career, through mentoring, advocacy, and opening doors.

We're also genuinely curious about new forms. Galleries, theatres, outdoor spaces, immersive installations, and the unexpected places that storytelling and music can live when they step off the screen.

Play and creativity

Play is serious work. We run sessions for children, young people, and the adults who work with them, putting creative play at the centre of how we learn and make.

Screen skills and storytelling

Practical masterclasses in animation, live action, sound design, and how to tell a story through image and sound. From students to seasoned professionals.

Women in film and TV

Mentoring, advocating, and opening doors for women working across directing, writing, design, and production. This runs through everything we do.

Immersive and new forms

Galleries, theatres, online, outdoors. We're genuinely curious about what happens when you take the work off the screen and put it somewhere unexpected.
