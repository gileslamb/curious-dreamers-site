The studio

Curious Dreamers

A Glasgow-based music and screen studio. We make animated series, immersive films, sound installations and creative education projects. Storytelling and music sit at the heart of everything we do.

The studio

It started with a lullaby

Hushabye Lullabye won double RTS awards for CBeebies and was voted one of the top 50 children's shows ever made by Guardian readers. Distance to the Moon picked up awards on the festival circuit. Beautiful things keep happening when we follow the music.

Story and music, together.

Sound and music aren't added at the end of our process. They grow alongside the story from the very beginning. We work across animation, live action, stop motion, and immersive formats, bringing a European artistic sensibility and a genuine love for the craft.

Friends and collaborators

We're lucky to work with brilliant people. BAFTA, RTS, WFTV, Screen Scotland, BFI, CBeebies, Channel 4, broadcasters, funders, and festival programmers who share our appetite for work that feels a bit different.

About

Sacha Kyle

Award-winning director, writer, and creative producer with 20+ years across animation, live action, theatre, comedy, and drama. A BAFTA member and WFTV Fellow, named an International Children's Media Fellow by the LSX Jacobs Foundation. Her animated series Hushabye Lullabye (CBeebies/BBC) won two RTS Awards and reached audiences worldwide. Deep expertise in early years development, play-based learning, and nurturing new creative talent.

About

Giles Lamb

Composer, sound designer, and educator with 25 years across film, television, games, installations, and immersive media. Work includes Valhalla Rising (BBC Films), Dead Island (Cannes Golden Lion), Tom Gates (Sky), and Hushabye Lullabye (CBeebies). Ten studio albums released. Teaches at the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland and the National Film and Television School. A background in neuroscience informs how he thinks about sound, emotion, and audience experience.

Curious Minds

Sharing what we know

Between us we have decades of experience making creative work, teaching, and figuring out what actually helps people grow. Curious Minds is where we share all of that. through workshops, masterclasses, and hands-on collaboration.

We run masterclasses in soundtrack composition, screen skills, creative storytelling, and play-based practice. We work with industry professionals, educators, students, and young people.

Championing women in film and television runs through everything we do. We support female talent across directing, writing, design, and production at every stage of their career.

Play and creativity

Play is serious work. We run sessions for children, young people, and the adults who work with them, putting creative play at the centre of how we learn and make.

Screen skills and storytelling

Practical masterclasses in animation, live action, sound design, and how to tell a story through image and sound. From students to seasoned professionals.

Women in film and TV

Mentoring, advocating, and opening doors for women working across directing, writing, design, and production. This runs through everything we do.

Immersive and new forms

Galleries, theatres, online, outdoors. We're genuinely curious about what happens when you take the work off the screen and put it somewhere unexpected.
